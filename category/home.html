Since February 2021, I am a CNRS researcher (chargée de recherche) at the university of Bordeaux. I am part of the Institut de Mathématiques de Bordeaux (IMB) and of the Lfant inria team. I am interested in lattice based cryptography, and more specifically in the hardness of algorithmic problems related to algebraically structured lattices.

From November 2019 to January 2021, I was a post-doctoral researcher in the COSIC team of KU Leuven, under the supervision of Frederik Vercauteren.

From September 2016 to October 2019, I was a PhD student in the LIP laboratory, ENS de Lyon, under the direction of Damien Stehlé. During my PhD, I worked on structured lattices, as well as cryptanalysis of obfuscators and multilinear maps.
